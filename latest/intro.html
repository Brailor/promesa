Getting Started

A promise library & concurency toolkit for Clojure and ClojureScript.

Setting up the dependency

deps.edn:

funcool/promesa {:mvn/version "9.1.536"}

Leiningen:

[funcool/promesa "9.1.536"]

Checking on the REPL

(require '[promesa.core :as p])

(->> (p/promise 1)
     (p/map inc)
     (deref)
;; => 2
